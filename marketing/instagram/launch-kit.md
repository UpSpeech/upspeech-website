# Instagram Launch Kit

Everything needed to actually open and run the account: profile setup, the
link-in-bio plan, highlight order, and finalized copy-paste captions. Captions
here are final (with hashtags); the `content-calendar.md` holds the schedule and
the longer rationale.

Read `../brand-voice.md` before posting. No clinical claims, no fluency-as-goal,
no mocking, no fear hooks.

## Profile setup

- **Avatar:** `exports/profile-avatar.png` (the logo glyph on navy; Instagram
  crops it to a circle, which is why the glyph is centered with room to spare).
- **Display name:** UpSpeech
- **Username:** @upspeech (or @upspeech.app if taken)
- **Category:** Health & wellness app / Medical & health
- **Bio (under 150 chars):**

  ```text
  Continuous support for stuttering therapy.
  Stutter-positive. Built with SLPs.
  Ask your therapist 👇
  ```

  (If you want zero emoji per the in-product rule, drop the 👇 and end with
  "Ask your therapist".)

- **Link:** one destination to start. Use the link-in-bio page below, or point
  straight at the for-patients page.

## Link-in-bio plan

Start with one link. When you need more than one, a simple list page beats a
third-party tool and keeps the brand. Suggested order:

1. **Ask your therapist about UpSpeech** -> `/for-patients`
2. **For therapists** -> `/for-slps`
3. **Download the app** -> App Store / Google Play
4. **What is stutter-positive?** -> the awareness explainer

## Highlight order (left to right)

Covers are built as `highlight-*`. Pin in this order so a new visitor reads the
story top to bottom:

1. `Practice` - what daily practice looks like
2. `Progress` - streaks, scores, weekly recaps
3. `Techniques` - easy onset, pacing, light contact, explained simply
4. `Stories` - consented patient and community wins
5. `Facts` - stutter-positive myth-busting
6. `For SLPs` - the clinician story (keep last; it's the B2B note)
