# Instagram Content Calendar

First four weeks, mapped to the exported assets. Captions are drafts; keep them
calm, encouraging, and within the sensitivity rules in
[`../brand-voice.md`](../brand-voice.md). No clinical claims, no fluency-as-goal.

Default weekly rhythm:

- **Monday** — Practice or Progress (start-of-week motivation).
- **Wednesday** — Education (carousel or stutter-positive post).
- **Friday** — Companion, scenario, or CTA.
- **Weekend** — stories: polls, checklists, fact drops.

Publish all six highlight covers before or during week 1.

## Reels (once recordings / companion animations exist)

- 10-15s: a single guided easy-onset rep, screen-recorded, calm voiceover.
- Companion reaction loop: the character nodding / celebrating a streak.
- "Myth in 15 seconds": one stutter-positive fact, captioned.
- Therapist angle: "the note wrote itself" screen recording (for SLPs).

Animate the strongest one or two stills per week, not the whole library. See
`visual-production-pipeline.md`.

## Caption pattern

1. Strong, calm first line (often the headline).
2. One real moment the reader recognizes.
3. How UpSpeech helps, plainly.
4. A practice or session takeaway.
5. One clear, low-pressure CTA.

Keep it speakable. No em-dashes. Five to eight specific hashtags.
